ILLUSTRATION OF LION

A lion is a large, powerful cat that belongs to the genus Panthera. It lives in Africa and India, where it is a top predator and a social animal. It forms groups called prides, which consist of a few males, several females, and their cubs. Male lions have a distinctive mane of hair around their necks, while female lions are smaller and more agile. Lions are known for their roar, which can be heard up to 8 km away. They hunt mostly large ungulates, such as zebras, wildebeests, and buffaloes, but they also scavenge and sometimes prey on humans. Lions are threatened by habitat loss, human-wildlife conflict, and poaching. A lion is a large, powerful cat that belongs to the genus Panthera.
